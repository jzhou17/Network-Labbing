Lab 2

Date: 3/9/2025

Tools used:
Aruba Central, PuTTY SSH Client

Gear used:
AOS-CX 6300 Switch

Goal:
Create 2 WLANs on the 6300M switch. One will be an Employee WLAN and the other will be an IoT WLAN. They should be on different networks and neither should be on the AP management network (That's bad practice).

On the IoT network, they should not be able to contact any devices on the private/internal network. The only access they should have is the Internet. The Employee WLAN can have access to any internal resources and the Internet, except gaming and sports related websites.

Note, you cannot configure anything beyond the 6300M switch.
